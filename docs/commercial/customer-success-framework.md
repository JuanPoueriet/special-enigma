# Customer Success Enterprise Framework

## Onboarding Operating Model

1. **Kick-off**: Requirements validation and compliance profile mapping (T0).
2. **Technical Setup**: Tenant provisioning (Shared/Schema/Dedicated) and fiscal certificate onboarding (T+2 days).
3. **Data Migration**: Legacy data import and reconciliation (T+7 days).
4. **UAT**: Client verification of CUJs (Critical User Journeys) (T+15 days).
5. **Go-Live**: Production activation and hypercare (T+21 days).

## Adoption & Health Scoring

- **Product Adoption Score (PAS)**: % of modules used vs licensed.
- **Activity Index**: DAU/MAU for critical roles (Sales, Finance, Warehouse).
- **Sentiment**: CSAT/NPS measured quarterly.

## Renewal & Expansion Playbooks

- **30-Day Check**: Initial value realization.
- **90-Day Review**: Business process optimization (O2C, P2P).
- **Renewal (T-90)**: Contractual and technical health audit.

## Metrics (NRR/Churn)

- **NRR Target**: > 115%
- **Logo Churn Target**: < 3%
- **Activation Time (TTV)**: < 30 days
